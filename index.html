About Us

  1. Established on 12 January 1957 as a Public Record Office.
  2. 1963 known as the National Archives of Malaysia.
  3. 1966 State Archives Act No.44, Year 1966 is approved.
  4. 1966 National Archives building in Petaling Jaya was inaugurated.
  5. 1982 National Archives to move to a new building in Jalan Duta, Kuala Lumpur.
  6. 2003 National Archives Act 2003 (Act 629) passed.
  7. 2004 National Archives of Malaysia is placed under the Ministry of Culture, Arts and Heritage.
